Crocs, Inc. is an American footwear company based in Broomfield, Colorado. Founded in 2002, Crocs is renowned for its foam footwear, often referred to as 'clogs.' The company is publicly traded on Nasdaq under the symbol CROX and is a component of the S&P 400. With a commitment to comfort and style, Crocs offers a diverse range of products, including bestsellers, men's, women's, and kids' collections, along with new arrivals and limited offers. As of 2021, Crocs has experienced substantial growth in revenue, net income, and assets, solidifying its position as a leader in the footwear industry.
